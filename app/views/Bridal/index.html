Fairy tale wedding or low key ceremony, your wedding day is one of the most precious memories you will treasure in your lifetime. Rebecca understands that your hair is integral to feeling a million dollars and will work with you to create your dream look for your big day. Understanding and getting to know the bride and the bridal party is key to this, so she will travel to you for a trial run weeks in advance of the wedding. During the trial she will talk to you and your party to learn about your likes and dislikes, assess your hair type and what kind of dresses you will be wearing. The trial is designed to inspire you and to reassure you that you will have your perfect look on the day itself.

A good place to start for ideas is to look in bridal magazine and online for styles that you like, so that Rebecca gets a taste of your hair vision. She will build on these ideas to personalise a style that suits you and your wedding theme.

With nothing left to chance, you can rest assured that you will feel radiant and confident in the spotlight.
